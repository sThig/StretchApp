Standing Pike

INSTRUCTIONS

Bend forward, hinging at the hips while trying to keep the back flat.

Do not try to touch your toes - instead, try to touch the ground 1-2 feet in front of your toes. This will help you hinge at the hips and not bend at the back.

When you can get decently below parallel with a flat back, then you can grab your calves and attempt to pull your head to your knees.

Note, when returning from the stretch, bend your knees and let your hips drop before, before lifting your body up. Stand up as if squatting.

Beginner

Forward bend with a flat back

Intermediate

When below parallel with a flat back grab your calves and pull your knees to your chest

Advanced

Pull your knees to your chest without using your arms to pull
